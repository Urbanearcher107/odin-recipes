Beef Pho with Brisket and Eye Round Steak

A light and fresh soup made with clear beef broth, rice noodles, beef, and an assortment of garnishes.

Ingridients

  • Beef broth
  • Yellow onion
  • Fresh ginger
  • 8 oz pack of dried rice vermicelli noodles
  • 1/2 lb beef brisket
  • 1/4 lb raw eye round
  • Green onions
  • Lime wedges
  • Herbs

Steps

    Make Broth

  1. Place a large dry pot over medium heat add the onion halves and ginger pieces and cook for 4 minutes
  2. Add the broth, water, coriander, clove, fish sauce, hoisin sauce, soy sauce, chili garlic paste, cinnamon stick and a pinch of fresh cracked salt and pepper to the pot. Bring to a slow boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes or more.

    3. Prepare Noodles

  3. prepare rice noodles according to package instructions. Once soft, drain, rinse and set aside

    4. Prepare Garnishes

  4. Ready the garnishes in individual small bowls for serving: cilantro, basil, green onions, bean sprouts, lime wedges and sliced chilies.

    5. Add Meat to Broth

  5. 1-5 minutes before the broth is done cooking, add desired meat to the broth to cook, just until done
  6. Discard the ginger, clove, cinnamon stick and onion pieces from the pot.

    7. Assemble

  7. Add cooked rice noodles to individual bowls and ladle hot broth over the top. Pile high with your favorite garnishes.
